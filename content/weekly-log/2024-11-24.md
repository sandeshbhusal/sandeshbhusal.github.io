I got this idea recently from a [blog I recently read](https://guergabo.substack.com/s/weekly-learnings). Although I am keep my thoughts to myself, I have come to the realization that I need to express myself more for a multitude of reasons:

- I really, _really_ need to get better at writing
- I keep forgetting things I've learned. Buying Obsidian did not help - I have a lot of notes, but they are .. discrete - each in its own corner, waiting to be connected.
- I need to keep a log of things I have actually learned. Sometimes, you might _think_ you've learned a lot over the week, but in reality you have not. This is a way to keep myself accountable.
- I keep writing about things in CS, your average algorithms and experiences, but I have a lot of other interests that I want to explore. This is a way to keep track of that.
